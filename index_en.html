Quality


Cut hairs – this is the best choice for extantion. Cut hairs have a good structure and you can count on natural beauty, maximum usage time.
Features of different sections of hair.
Cut hairs are straight and curly, they are collected in Ukraine and neighboring countries. They are much thinner and smaller in weight than Asian ones. The South-Russian hairs are heavier than the Slavic ones, they are cut off in Kazakhstan, Uzbekistan and other countries. Hairs from one donor have the same structure. Using materials of poor quality affects the result of work, so we recommend not to save on consumables. Each cut is formed as follows: after receipt, the raw material is processed and disinfected. The material is sorted by type and color, quality, degree of waviness, softness. If high-quality raw materials are used, which is competently processed, artificial curls look at the client as well as their own. When observing the rules of wearing and care, our materials can be used repeatedly. Our company offers you a wide choice of hair of different structure.

South-Russian hairs
Many beauty salons and hairdressers, in order to satisfy the desire of their customers, are forced to make extantion natural hair with another structure, as it is very difficult to find quality natural Slavic hair. In addition, tthe price of Slavic hairs is more than 1,5-2,5 times higher than the price of hair of South-Russian quality. Therefore Modo hairs expert offers natural South-Russian hairs at a market price, the quality of which is as close as possible to the Slavic. All goods are carefully checked for quality and stored in ideally prepared conditions. The company constantly replenishes its stocks with new products and offers a wide range of different colors of South-Russian hair. At purchase hairs pass strict quality control in a special laboratory, which guarantees an impeccable look of the hairs after extantion and long term of wearing for your customers.

Slavic hairs
Slavik hairs is considered the most valuable type of hairs used for extention. Their main suppliers are Ukraine and Central Russia. Slavic hairs are thin, soft, silky curls. It is good for wearing. The main advantage of Slavic hair is that they easily give in processing chemical compounds and color, after washing and drying the hair as much as possible retain its shape and properties. We can use them can repeatedly. But price of these hairs more expensive than South-Russian hair in 1,5-2,5 times.
