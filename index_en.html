Quality


Hair in slices is the best choice for extensions. MODO slices have a good structure and you can rely on natural beauty and maximum terms of use, provided the right choice.
Our company offers unpainted and dyed hair of LUX and GOLD quality. For dyeing hair, we use high quality raw materials. For all hair with a shade above 08 are used exclusively unpainted hair from one donor (quality LUX) in order to obtain a more even bleaching and staining in the right tone. For all hair with a shade below 08, factory raw material (GOLD quality) can be used, while the quality remains the same. Factory raw materials are hair from several donors and are easier to find, therefore they are somewhat cheaper than from a single donor. Therefore, the cost of hair quality GOLD slightly lower LUX.
BASIC RULE FOR HAIR SELECTION
The basic rule for hair selection is very simple. In independence from country of origin and cost of hair choose hair similar in structure to yours, pay attention to the uniformity of hair coloring throughout the length, and also on the quality of the tips.
Hair slices are straight, wavy and curly. Also, when choosing a hair, it is necessary to take into account their weight, porosity and degree of rigidity. Hair can be cut off from one donor (from one head) or from several donors (factory). In difference of factory hair in a cut from one donor (from one head) hairs of the same structure. The use of materials of poor quality affects the result of work, therefore we recommend not to save on consumables. Each slice is formed as follows: after receipt, the raw materials are processed and disinfected. The material is sorted by type and color, quality, degree of waviness, softness. If you use high-quality raw materials that are properly processed, artificial curls look on the client no worse than your own. With the observance of the rules of wearing and care, our hair for a long time retains the appearance and can be reused. Our company offers you a wide selection of hair slices of different structure.

The whole truth about South Russian hair
All South Russian hair sellers are not able to give an unequivocal answer in which place they buy this hair. At the same time, the cost of these so-called southern Russian hair often exceeds $150-$250/100 grams (60cm). In fact, all this hair is nothing more than good quality hair imported from Asia. But since the client cannot verify and identify the country of origin, many beauty salons and craftsmen by extension, they say that the hair is southern Russian, thus justifying its inflated prices.

Slavic hair
Slavic hair is mistakenly considered the most valuable type of hair used for extension. However, we already wrote above that ideal hair extensions are those that suit you in a structure and are qualitatively colored. Slavic hair has its own specifics and it is difficult to find them in large quantities. This is their value. and accordingly high cost (2-3 times higher than the cost of Asian hair). The main suppliers are Ukraine and Central Russia. It is believed that Slavic hair is thin, soft and silky, easy to process chemical compositions and color, after washing and drying the hair as much as possible retain their shape and properties. However, it should be noted that the time of use of hair depends on their quality, and not on the country of origin. Very often beauty salons and masters of hair extensions sell Asian hair for the price of Slavic hair. Also worth noting that Slavic hair with a length of 65 cm is a rarity, a Slav blond is also rare.
Collaborate with professionals. Our company recommends you before buying hair for extensions. consult several hair extension masters or hair sellers. This will help you save a lot of money.
